Why BTS?

BTS is a Grammy-nominated South Korean group that has been capturing hearts of millions of fans globally since their debut on June 13, 2013. The seven members of BTS are RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V, and Jung Kook.

The group is known for their authentic and self-produced music, top-notch performances, and the way they interact with their fans, named ARMY. BTS has broken countless world records, have been recognized with numerous prestigious awards, and have performed multiple sold-out stadium shows around the world. BTS joined UNICEF in 2017 for their 'Love Myself' campaign, pursuing love and abetter place to live for all.

With all that BTS has done and continue to do, they have shown that music transcends language.

Save Me

"Save Me" is the third track from BTS's first compilation album 'The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever.' A tropical house and electropop track, the song is about how someone's love can go a long way in saving a person from the depths of darkness.

Spring Day

"봄날" (Spring Day) is the title track to BTS's repackaged album 'You Never Walk Alone." Co-written by members RM and SUGA, the song is about missing someone and trying to do anything to be able to reach them.
